Brief 

Hello! 
I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University (In cooperation with Prof Jia Jia).
Currently, I focus on the topic of Human-AI Interaction.
My research interests include Multimedia, Artificial Intelligence, Optimization and their applications. My past research interests include Theory of Evolutionary Algorithms.
